## Full Description (4000 characters max)

Never forget an item at the store again! Grocery List is a simple, fast, and beautiful app to manage your shopping lists.

**Key Features:**

- Add items with name, quantity, and category
- Organize items by category (Fruits, Vegetables, Dairy, Meat, Household, General)
- Mark items as purchased with a simple tap
- Track your shopping progress with a visual progress bar
- Swipe left to quickly delete items
- Filter by category to focus on specific sections
- Hide completed items to see what's left
- Beautiful Material Design 3 interface
- Works offline - no account required
- Automatic cloud backup with Google

**Why Grocery List?**

- Fast & Lightweight: Opens instantly, no bloat
- Privacy First: All data stays on your device
- No Ads: Clean, distraction-free experience
- No Account Required: Start using immediately
- Modern Design: Material You support with dynamic colors

**Perfect for:**
- Weekly grocery shopping
- Quick trips to the store
- Meal planning
- Household supply tracking

Download now and make your shopping trips more organized!
